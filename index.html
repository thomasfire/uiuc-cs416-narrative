Tough to Rent?

For many years, Hong Kong has been notorious for its ridiculously small and expensive housing. Yet more, the rental situation is barely improving despite all the global turmoil Hong Kong getting intertwined with.

Hover over the points to know the typical monthly rent per square meter in each of the Class x Territory.

Colors notation: Hong Kong Island — blue; Kowloon — yellow to red; New Territories — green. The darker the color, the lower the class of the flat.

Classes: A — below 40 sq.m., B — 40-69.9 sq.m., C — 70-99.9 sq.m., D — 100-159.9 sq.m., E — more than 160 sq.m.
